Consulting

Healthcare innovation through clinical evidence and strategy.

Jennifer translates biomedical science into clear, decision-ready insights for cross-functional teams. By combining clinical research expertise and advanced analytics, she generates evidence and communicates complex findings to inform strategy, improve healthcare decisions, and strengthen patient outcomes. Engagements are selective and built on genuine collaboration.

Areas of Expertise

Grant and Scientific Writing
Research proposals, NIH and CIHR-style grants, technical white papers, and peer-reviewed manuscripts.
Advanced Analytics and Bioinformatics
Multi-omics data analysis and integration spanning genomics, proteomics, metabolomics, and metagenomics. Custom pipeline development using industry-standard bioinformatics tools. Statistical modelling, machine learning, and reproducible workflow design for complex biological datasets.
Clinical Research and Strategy
Study design, protocol development, randomised controlled trial coordination, secondary analysis, and regulatory submissions. Experienced across multi-site international clinical programmes spanning multiple countries.
Scientific Communication and Knowledge Translation
Translating complex science into clear strategies, clinician communications, and funded outcomes for biotech, health technology, and academic clients. Tailored for audiences ranging from bench scientists to boards and investors.
Brand Strategy
Positioning, data storytelling, and product narratives for early-stage life science and health ventures. Helping founders and scientists articulate their vision with clarity across web, pitch, and investor materials.
Research and Evidence Strategy
Advising organisations on how to use evidence strategically for positioning, policy, funding, and impact. Connecting scientific findings to business and social outcomes.
